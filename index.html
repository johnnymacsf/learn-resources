About Us

Getting started in learning how to code may seem challenging and daunting, so we are here to provide a catalog of great resources to choose from, either for beginners just starting out or for those experienced and looking for new things to learn or sharpen their skills! To learn something new each day is a great lifelong goal to have, and we're here to help you!

Listed below is a catalog of resource links, videos, and example projects that help you both learn and practice your skills in computer programming. If you are a beginner we recommend checking out the beginner section first and following the roadmaps provided.

If you are already experienced in computer programming but looking to learn a new language or skill, we have great sources for that as well!
